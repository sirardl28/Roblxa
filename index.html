ABOUT US

Roblxa was founded in 2024. The founders were Lily Sirard and Kate Spillane. The main headquarters are located in Walpole, Massachusetts. The first couple hundred builders of Roblxa were all developers, investors, testers, and friends of the founders. Our project straddles several rapidly-developing aspects of internet entertainment: virtual worlds, casual gaming, and player-constructed content. Roblxa is free to play and safe for kids aged 4+. Our product remains under development and changes to the apps and product happen frequently. We deeply value community feedback as we continue to make Roblox into a platform that will excite the imagination of the young and the young at heart
