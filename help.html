FINDING GAMES

You can use the search bar that is in the app that is at the top of the screen to look up games, people or anything that has to do with the app right in that search bar(this does not include help, and settings). To use the search bar you tap the icon at the top of the screen. Enter a title of a game or a keyword in the search bar, this includes people's usernames.

APPEALING BANS

An account suspension is a small action that prevents the user's account from accessing Roblxa services, and is given when the user is found to be violating the Terms of Use. Suspensions can take the form of a warning which can be resolved by the user immediately, a ban in which the account is suspended for a period of time, or an account deletion in which the account is permanently banned. When an account is suspended, all pages on the Roblox website redirect to the "not approved" page where a description of the offense and moderation is shown. Bans can last from 1 day to permanently banned.
